Omissions
  • The actual number of executions in China is a state secret; Amnesty International estimates 'thousands' rather than providing a precise figure.
  • China's execution totals are excluded from official global statistics precisely because the true extent remains unknown.
  • The 'more than 1,000' figure cited is likely a conservative estimate; actual numbers are believed to be significantly higher (in the thousands).
  • The source cited (Amnesty International) publishes this data with a lag; the most recent available data at the time of the speech would be from 2024 (published April 2025).
