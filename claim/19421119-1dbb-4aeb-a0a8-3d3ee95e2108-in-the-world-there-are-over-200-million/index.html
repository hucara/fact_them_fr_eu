Omissions
  • The current figure for FGM is 230 million (UNICEF 2024), not just 'over 200 million' - representing a 15% increase from 2016 when the 200 million figure was first reported.
  • The claim does not specify whether it refers to FGM alone or both FGM and child marriage combined. If referring to both practices, the figure would be substantially higher (child marriage alone affects 640 million).
  • The UNICEF data referenced was published in March 2024, prior to the session date of March 11, 2026.
