The food basket for a Swedish two-child family has become approximately 3,000 Swedish kronor more expensive since 2022
75% confidence
AgricultureSweden
Omissions
  • The claim was made in February 2026, but the most recent detailed data found is from February 2024
  • The largest increase occurred in 2022-2023 (approximately 900 SEK), with price increases slowing significantly in 2024 (around 2.8% according to CPI data)
  • The data comes from Swedbank's 'matkasse' index which is a specific basket of goods, not an official government statistic
  • Food price inflation in Sweden peaked in 2022-2023 and has moderated considerably since then
