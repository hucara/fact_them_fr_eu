24% of LGBT community members state they have undergone some form of conversion practice
95% confidence
EqualityEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The 24% figure corresponds specifically to the 2023 FRA survey, not to data from 2025 or 2026. The rate is even higher for trans and non-binary people (32%) and intersex people (28%). The report also notes that only 6 of the 27 EU Member States have completely banned these practices.
