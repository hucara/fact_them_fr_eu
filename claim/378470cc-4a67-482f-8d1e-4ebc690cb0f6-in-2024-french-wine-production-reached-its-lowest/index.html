Omissions
  • The specific comparison is to 1957, not 1950 exactly. Agreste reported 39.3 million hectoliters for 2024, which was the lowest since 1957.
  • The production decline was 22% lower than 2023 and 15% below the five-year average, according to official French agricultural statistics (Agreste).
  • The claim was made in February 2026, and while the underlying 2024 data is correct, the MEP cited a commonly rounded figure ('since 1950' vs. the more precise 'since 1957').
  • Sources used are secondary (news articles citing Agreste) rather than the primary Agreste publication itself.
