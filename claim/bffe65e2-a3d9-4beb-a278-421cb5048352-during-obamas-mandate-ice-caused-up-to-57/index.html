During Obama's mandate, ICE caused up to 57 deaths in its work capturing violent illegal immigrants.
75% confidence
MigrationNorth America
Omissions
  • The claim omits that these 56 deaths occurred while individuals were in ICE detention/custody, not during enforcement operations or 'capturing violent illegal immigrants'
  • The deaths were largely attributed to poor medical care, suicide, or health complications while detained, not violence during arrest operations
  • The sources report 56 deaths, not 57
  • No distinction is made between deaths of violent criminals versus non-violent immigrants in custody
  • The ACLU report (published 2016) found that ICE failed to adequately investigate or prevent these deaths
