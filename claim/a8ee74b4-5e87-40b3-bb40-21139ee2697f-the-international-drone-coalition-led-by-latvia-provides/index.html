Omissions
  • The figure of 1 billion significantly understates the Drone Coalition's actual funding, which reached €1.8 billion in 2024 alone
  • The claim conflates Latvia's total bilateral aid to Ukraine (nearly €1 billion since February 2022) with the International Drone Coalition's collective funding
  • The Drone Coalition is co-coordinated by Latvia and the United Kingdom, not solely led by Latvia
  • The coalition allocated €1.8 billion in 2024 and plans €2.75 billion in 2025, totaling €4.5 billion over two years
