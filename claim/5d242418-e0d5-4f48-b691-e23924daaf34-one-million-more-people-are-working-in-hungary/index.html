One million more people are working in Hungary than at the beginning of the decade
75% confidence
EconomyHungary
Omissions
  • The exact employment figure for the beginning of 2020 is not directly cited in the available sources, though KSH data confirms the magnitude of increase is approximately one million
  • The data refers to employed persons aged 15-74, which is the standard Labour Force Survey definition used by KSH
  • Sources from 2025/2026 were published after the session date but contain data from periods before 2026-01-22
  • The Statistical Pocketbook of Hungary 2021 notes that 2020 and 2021 data are not fully comparable with figures from previous periods due to methodology changes
