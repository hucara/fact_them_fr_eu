At least 40% of all cancer cases could be prevented with adequate measures
95% confidence
HealthInternational
Omissions
  • The WHO states a broader range of 30-50% as preventable, not just 'at least 40%'. The 40% figure represents the upper end of more recent specific estimates, while the WHO's general position is the 30-50% range.
  • The February 2026 WHO report specifically states 'up to four in ten' (40%) could be prevented, which supports but slightly narrows the claim.
