Omissions
  • The 90% figure refers to global data from IRENA covering 2010-2024 (14 years), not strictly a single decade. The claim was made without citing a specific source.
  • The IRENA source used (published 2025) was not yet available at the time of the speech, though the data trends were well-established from earlier reports. IRENA's 2023 report also showed consistent dramatic cost declines.
  • The claim does not specify that this refers to the Levelized Cost of Energy (LCOE) or whether it includes total installed costs vs. electricity generation costs.
