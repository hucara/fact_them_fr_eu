Omissions
  • The official EU proposal specifies 'at least 80%' of costs, not 'up to 100%'
  • Generic medicines would bear around 60% of EPR costs according to industry estimates, which is disproportionate to their market share but not 100%
  • The claim was made on the same day as the relevant European Parliament debate (2026-03-25) on Urban Wastewater Treatment Directive's impact on the pharmaceutical sector
  • The 'polluter pays principle' is cited as the basis for the 80% minimum cost allocation
