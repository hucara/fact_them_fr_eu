Omissions
  • The figure represents a weighted average across primary (8%) and post-primary (3.6%) levels, not a uniform percentage across all school levels
  • Data is from the 2023/24 or 2024/25 school year - the MEP could have known this data at the time of the session
  • The statistic distinguishes between primary and post-primary levels, which show significantly different participation rates (8% vs 3.6%)
  • Data covers the 26 counties of the Republic of Ireland only
