1.2 million children in Romania are excluded from social inclusion
72% confidence
Social PolicyRomania
Omissions
  • The MEP used the term 'social exclusion' but the official Eurostat metric is 'at risk of poverty or social exclusion' (AROPE), which is a broader indicator that includes poverty risk, severe material and social deprivation, and household work intensity
  • The 1.2 million figure represents children at risk of poverty or social exclusion, not necessarily fully socially excluded
  • Romania actually registered the largest decrease (-5.2 percentage points) in this indicator in 2024 compared with 2023, which is positive context that was omitted
  • The data comes from 2024 (published May 2025), which is the most recent available before the session date
  • The percentage of children at risk in Romania was 32.6% in 2024, down from 37.8% in 2023
