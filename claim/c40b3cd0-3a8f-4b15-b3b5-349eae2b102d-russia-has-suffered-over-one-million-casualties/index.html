Omissions
  • The figure includes killed, wounded, and missing, not just deaths
  • Sources vary significantly: Ukrainian military claims vs. UN-confirmed data show large discrepancy
  • The CSIS source (1.2 million casualties) is an estimate from a think tank, not official government data
  • Casualty figures in active conflicts are inherently uncertain and subject to significant estimation error
  • The MEP did not specify that these are estimates rather than confirmed figures
