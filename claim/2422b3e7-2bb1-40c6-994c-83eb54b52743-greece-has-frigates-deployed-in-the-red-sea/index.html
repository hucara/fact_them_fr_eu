Greece has frigates deployed in the Red Sea
92% confidence

Omissions
  • The Greek frigates are deployed as part of the EU-led Operation ASPIDES (Greek for 'shield'), not an independent Greek operation
  • Operation ASPIDES began in February 2024 following Houthi attacks on shipping in the Red Sea
  • The deployment includes multiple rotations of Greek MEKO 200HN frigates (Hydra, Spetsai)
  • Sources found are from 2024, but the operation extension through February 2027 was confirmed by more recent sources
