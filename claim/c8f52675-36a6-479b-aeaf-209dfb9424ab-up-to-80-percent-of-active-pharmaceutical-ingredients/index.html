Up to 80 percent of active pharmaceutical ingredients come to Europe from China and India.
92% confidence
HealthEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The 'up to 80%' figure represents the upper bound of estimates, with some sources citing lower figures in the 60-70% range
  • This dependency figure includes both China and India combined, not individually
  • The EESC document was published in October 2024, based on data from 2023-2024
  • The European Commission Staff Working Document SWD(2025) 263 from September 2025 cites similar dependency figures for third-country API sourcing
Sources
  • PrimaryEuropean Economic and Social Committee (EESC) - Opinion on Securing Europe's medicine supplyAccording to current data, up to 80% of APIs used in Europe and about 40% of finished medicines sold in Europe come from China or India
  • PrimaryEuropean Commission - Staff Working Document SWD(2025) 263 finalDocument analyzing EU pharmaceutical dependencies and supply chain vulnerabilities including reliance on third-country API sourcing
  • AcademicPharmacia Journal - The European Union's dependence on imports of active pharmaceutical ingredientsUp to 80% of the chemical substances required for pharmaceutical production in the EU are sourced or synthesized outside its territory
