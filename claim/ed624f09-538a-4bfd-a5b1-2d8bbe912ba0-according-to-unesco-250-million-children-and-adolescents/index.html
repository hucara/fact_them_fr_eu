Omissions
  • The 250 million figure reflects 2023 data published in September 2023, not the most recent available data as of February 2026
  • UNESCO's 2025 SDG 4 Scorecard and 2025 dashboard reported 272 million out-of-school children (data from 2023, published in 2025)
  • UNESCO's 2024 Global Education Monitoring Report indicated 251 million children and youth were out of school
  • The current figure of 272-273 million represents an increase of approximately 21-23 million compared to earlier estimates
  • The MEP used outdated statistics that underrepresent the actual magnitude of the global out-of-school crisis
