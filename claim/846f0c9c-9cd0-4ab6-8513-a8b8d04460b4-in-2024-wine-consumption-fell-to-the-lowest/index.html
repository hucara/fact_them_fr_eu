Omissions
  • The claim conflates global wine consumption data with EU-specific data. The 'lowest since 1961' figure refers to global consumption (214.2 million hectolitres), not EU consumption. The EU represents a major portion of global consumption but the claim should clarify this is worldwide data. France alone consumed 23.0 million hectolitres in 2024.
  • Source (The Guardian article) was published on April 15, 2025, after the session date of February 9, 2026 - this is acceptable as it reports on 2024 data which the MEP could have known from earlier OIV releases.
