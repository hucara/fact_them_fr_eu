Omissions
  • The precise calculation yields approximately 7,397 cases per day (2.7 million divided by 365 days), so the figure of 7,500 represents a rounded estimate.
  • Data refers to the year 2024, which was the most recent complete year available at the time of the speech.
  • Figure applies to the 27 EU Member States (EU27, post-Brexit).
