The claimed gasoline price of 'over 2 euros per liter' for the EU is factually incorrect. The actual EU average gasoline price in 2025 was approximately €1.55 per liter, representing an overstatement of about 29%.
Omissions
  • The claim fails to distinguish between EU average prices and specific high-price member states; some Nordic countries have prices above €2/L but this is not representative of the EU as a whole.
  • The politician did not cite a source for this claim.
  • The European Commission's Weekly Oil Bulletin provides official weekly data showing EU average prices consistently below €2/L throughout 2024-2025.
