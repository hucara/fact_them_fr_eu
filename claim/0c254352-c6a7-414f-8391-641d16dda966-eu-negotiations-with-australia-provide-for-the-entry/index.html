Omissions
  • The agreement specifies that the 30.600 tonnes of beef will be implemented progressively over 10 years
  • For sheep/goat meat, the total quota is 25.000 tonnes CWE (carcass weight equivalent), not more than 30.000 tonnes as the speaker claims
  • The exact figures come from the tariff rate quota established in the EU-Australia agreement
  • The news source indailysa.com.au (24/03/2026) was published one day before the parliamentary session
