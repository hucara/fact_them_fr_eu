Deposit guarantee systems offer protection of up to 100,000 euros per depositor
98% confidence
EconomyEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The protection applies per depositor per credit institution (bank), meaning a depositor can have multiple protected deposits at different banks.
  • The €100,000 limit applies to the aggregate deposits of each depositor at one institution.
  • Temporary high balances (e.g., from real estate transactions) may be protected above €100,000 for up to 3 months.
