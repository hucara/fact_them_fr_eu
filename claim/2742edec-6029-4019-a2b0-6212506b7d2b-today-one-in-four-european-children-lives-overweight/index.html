Omissions
  • The data refers specifically to children aged 6-9 years, not all European children
  • The WHO data covers 37 European countries (including non-EU countries), not exclusively EU Member States
  • The data period is 2022-2024, not 2026 specifically
  • The WHO report was published in November 2025, which was available before the March 2026 claim date
  • The data shows wide variation across countries - not all countries have 25% rates
