Social workers in Bulgaria earn 600 euros per month
85% confidence
Social PolicyBulgaria
Omissions
  • The claim omits that social worker salaries in Bulgaria were significantly increased in 2024 following months of protests
  • The average social worker salary is approximately 1,074 euros per month, nearly double the claimed 600 euros
  • Newly appointed social workers have a minimum starting salary of BGN 1,400 (approximately 715 euros), higher than the claimed amount
  • The 600 euro figure is close to Bulgaria's general minimum wage (620.20 euros from January 2026), not specific to social workers
