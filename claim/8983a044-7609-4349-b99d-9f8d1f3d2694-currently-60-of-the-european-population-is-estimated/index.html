Currently 60% of the European population is estimated to be overweight or affected by obesity.
85% confidence
HealthEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The 60% figure appears to combine pre-obese (overweight, BMI 25-29.9) and obese (BMI ≥ 30) categories, which is methodologically correct but requires clarification that this refers to adults aged 18+, not the total European population including children
  • The claim does not specify that these statistics typically exclude children and refer to the adult population (aged 16 or 18+)
  • Data used is from 2022, as more recent comprehensive EU-wide data was not yet available at the time of the March 2026 claim
  • The source (Eurostat) was not cited by the MEP
