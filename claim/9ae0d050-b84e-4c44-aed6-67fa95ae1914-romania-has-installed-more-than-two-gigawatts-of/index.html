Omissions
  • The exact publication date of the source reporting the 2 GW milestone could not be verified within search limits
  • The claim implies recent data (March 2026), but the 2 GW milestone was reportedly reached in August of the previous year (likely 2025), meaning the figure could be higher by March 2026
  • Nuclear reactor capacity is reported in gross (720 MW each) vs net terms (approximately 650 MW each); combined net capacity is approximately 1.3 GW
  • Prosumer capacity includes both installed and operational capacity, which may differ from connected capacity
  • The doubling in 14 months suggests rapid growth, but starting baseline from end 2021 is not precisely verified
