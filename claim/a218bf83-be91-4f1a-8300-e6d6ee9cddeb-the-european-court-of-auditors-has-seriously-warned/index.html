The European Court of Auditors has seriously warned about continuous misuse of EU funds multiple times.
92% confidence
Internal AffairsEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The ECA's warnings specifically refer to 'errors' and 'irregularities' rather than deliberate 'misuse' or fraud - the majority are administrative errors rather than intentional misuse
  • The ECA has also noted improvements in some areas of financial management alongside continued concerns
  • The error rates cited (2.7%-5.6%) represent a minority of total EU spending, not the majority
  • The ECA continues to approve the reliability of EU accounts each year despite these concerns
