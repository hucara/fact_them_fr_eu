Omissions
  • The most recent available data (2024) from UN OCHA's Global Humanitarian Overview indicates that the Middle East and North Africa region, which includes major conflict zones, accounted for approximately 30% of global humanitarian funding requirements, not 80%. The search results did not reveal any official statistic supporting the 80% figure for conflict-related humanitarian assistance.
  • The session date of 2026-01-19 is in the future; verification relies on data available up to that point.
  • A statistic showing 'close to 80 per cent' was found but it referred to multi-year funding recorded before 2025, not to conflict victims.
