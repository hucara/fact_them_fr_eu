Omissions
  • The 60% claim for housing prices may be based on 2025 data (Q2 2025: 60.5%), but the most recent official data widely available at the time of the session (Q4 2024) indicated 55.4%
  • The 'almost 30%' figure for rents is a slight overestimation: in Q2 2025 it was 28.8% and in Q4 2024 it was 26.7%
  • The exact reference period is not specified (the increase varies depending on the 2024-2025 quarter used)
  • Eurostat data is published several months after the period it covers
