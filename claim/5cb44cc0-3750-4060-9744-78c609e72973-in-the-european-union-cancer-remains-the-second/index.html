In the European Union, cancer remains the second main cause of mortality, with over 2.7 million new cases annually and almost 1.3 million deaths.

Omissions
  • The figure of 2.7 million new cases refers specifically to 2024 data published by the Joint Research Centre in December 2025
  • The mortality figure of almost 1.3 million is a 2025 projection from Annals of Oncology (1.28 million deaths), slightly higher than the 1.1-1.14 million deaths reported for 2021-2022 by Eurostat
  • The second leading cause claim refers to death statistics from 2021-2022 where cancer accounted for 21.6%-22.3% of all deaths versus circulatory diseases at 32%-32.8%
