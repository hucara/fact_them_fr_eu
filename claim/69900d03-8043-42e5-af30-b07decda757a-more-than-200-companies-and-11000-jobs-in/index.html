Omissions
  • The data specifically refers to offshore wind energy, not the entire wind energy sector (which would include onshore wind). The claim omits the 'offshore' qualifier which constitutes the majority of Belgium's wind energy industry.
  • The figure of 230 companies exceeds the claimed 'more than 200' by a significant margin (15% higher).
  • There are conflicting projections suggesting 15,000-16,000 jobs in the future, though 11,000 represents the current baseline figure.
  • Source publication date is not explicitly stated on the Blue Cluster page, though the data appears to reflect recent (2023-2024) statistics.
