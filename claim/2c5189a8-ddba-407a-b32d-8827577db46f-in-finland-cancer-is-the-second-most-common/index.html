Omissions
  • The claim refers to 2024 data which may have been preliminary statistics at the time of the February 2026 speech, as official causes of death statistics are typically published with a 6-12 month delay
  • The OECD figure of 21% refers specifically to premature deaths (before age 75), not all deaths
  • Primary data from Statistics Finland official release for full-year 2024 could not be located to confirm the exact 23% figure for all-cause mortality
