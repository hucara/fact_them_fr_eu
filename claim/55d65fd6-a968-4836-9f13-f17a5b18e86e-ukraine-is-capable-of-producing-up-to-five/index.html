Omissions
  • The 5 million figure refers specifically to FPV (First Person View) drones, not all types of drones.
  • The EU production comparison lacks specific data support - no authoritative source was found confirming the EU produces only 500,000 drones annually.
  • The 5 million capacity figure represents production capacity rather than actual output - actual production in 2024 was 2.2 million drones according to Zelensky.
  • The claim conflates production capacity with actual production figures.
  • The comparison between Ukraine and EU may not be comparing equivalent categories of drones (military vs. civilian, FPV vs. other types).
