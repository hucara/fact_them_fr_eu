Omissions
  • The speaker does not specify that the full-scale invasion began on February 24, 2022, which is the commonly referenced start date for the current phase of the war. Some sources consider the broader Russo-Ukrainian conflict to have started in February 2014 with the annexation of Crimea, which would make the duration even longer (over 12 years). The claim of 'over four years' is therefore accurate for the full-scale invasion period and conservative if considering the broader conflict.
