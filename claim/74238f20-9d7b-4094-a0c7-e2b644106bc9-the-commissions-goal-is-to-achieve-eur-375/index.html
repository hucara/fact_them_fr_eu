Confirmed
The Commission's goal is to achieve EUR 37.5 billion in cost reductions for European businesses.
95% confidence
EconomyEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The EUR 37.5 billion target represents savings by the end of the Commission's mandate in 2029, not an immediate achievement.
  • The target is based on a 25% reduction in administrative burden for all businesses and 35% for SMEs.
  • The claim was made in the context of ten 'omnibus' packages that have been presented, which the Commission stated would reduce costs by EUR 15 billion once implemented.
  • The sources confirming this target were published in 2025, before the claim date of March 9, 2026.
