The International Monetary Fund estimated that internal barriers are equivalent to a 44% tariff.
95% confidence
EconomyEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The IMF estimate of 44% specifically refers to manufacturing sectors; the figure for services is substantially higher at approximately 110%.
  • The IMF used the phrase 'might be as high as' indicating this is an upper-bound estimate rather than a precise figure.
  • The underlying data refers to the situation as of 2020, though published in IMF reports from 2024.
