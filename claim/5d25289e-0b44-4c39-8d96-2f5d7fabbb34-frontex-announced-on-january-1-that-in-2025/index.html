Omissions
  • The actual Frontex announcement was published on January 15, 2026, not January 1, 2026 as claimed.
  • Frontex uses the term 'irregular border crossings' rather than 'illegal entries' - these are related but distinct concepts in migration statistics.
  • The 178,000 figure represents a 26% decrease from 2024 and is the lowest number since 2021, which provides important context about the downward trend.
