Omissions
  • The €300 billion figure represents 'mobilized investments' through financial instruments (loans, guarantees, grants, and blended finance), not direct spending
  • The target was originally set for 2027 but was reached ahead of schedule in October 2025
  • The announcement of reaching the €300 billion target was made in October 2025, which was before the March 2026 claim date but the data would have been available
  • The European Commission press release uses 'mobilised' and 'investment target' terminology, not 'spent'
