Omissions
  • The speaker omitted that current estimates from FEANTSA (the primary European organization tracking homelessness) indicate over 1.2 million homeless people, which is significantly higher than the 1 million figure cited.
  • The most recent data shows homelessness increased by 43% in 2024, a critical context for understanding the severity of the crisis.
  • The FEANTSA figure of 1.2 million includes approximately 400,000 children, highlighting the disproportionate impact on families.
  • The Il Sole 24 Ore article was published on December 4, 2024, which is before the session date of 2026-01-22.
