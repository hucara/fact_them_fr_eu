80% of basic medicines used in Europe depend on raw materials and production from outside the European Union.
35% confidence
HealthEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The claim lacks specificity on what constitutes 'basic medicines' (generic drugs, essential medicines, antibiotics, etc.)
  • No distinction is made between dependency on raw materials versus dependency on finished production
  • The claim does not specify the source or methodology for the 80% figure
  • Available pharmaceutical trade data (EFPIA) shows significantly lower import dependency for total pharmaceutical consumption (24.7% from outside Europe)
