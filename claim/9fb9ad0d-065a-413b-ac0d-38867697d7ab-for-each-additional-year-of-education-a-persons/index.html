For each additional year of education, a person's lifetime earnings increase by 10%.
92% confidence
EconomyInternational
Omissions
  • The 10% figure represents a global average that masks significant variation by country, region, gender, and education level
  • More recent World Bank reviews cite the figure as approximately 9% rather than exactly 10%
  • Returns vary significantly between developed and developing countries
  • The data represents long-term averages over decades rather than a specific recent year
