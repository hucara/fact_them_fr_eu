Omissions
  • The 34% figure cited refers specifically to French overseas territories (DROM-COM), not to France as a whole
  • For mainland France, the same BVA survey from November 2023 shows 22% of caregivers reported difficulties accessing care/medicines
  • The data comes from a survey of caregivers (aidants), not directly from patients
  • The survey was conducted in November 2023 and published in February 2024 - the MEP could have known this data at the time of the claim
  • The source is a joint publication by the Ligue nationale contre le cancer and BVA, not a government statistics office
Sources
