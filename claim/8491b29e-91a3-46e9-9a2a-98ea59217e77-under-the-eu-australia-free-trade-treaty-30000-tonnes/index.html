Under the EU-Australia free trade treaty, 30,000 tonnes of beef, 25,000 tonnes of sheep meat, 35,000 tonnes of sugar, milk powder and butter will be imported
92% confidence
AgricultureEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The beef quota is actually 30,600 tonnes (carcass weight equivalent), not 30,000 tonnes as stated - a slight underestimation of 600 tonnes (2%).
  • The specific quotas for dairy are: 8,000 tonnes for skimmed milk powder and 5,000 tonnes for butter.
  • The beef quota is phased in over 10 years, not available all at once at entry into force.
  • Source is official European Commission trade policy documentation, accessible to the MEP.
