  • The claim attributes the figure to 'UN sources' but the specific 35,000+ number appears primarily in media reports citing documents, not direct UN OHCHR published death tolls.
  • The MEP could not have known the February 2026 OHCHR press release at the time of the March 11, 2026 speech, but could have known the January 2026 OHCHR statements.
