Omissions
  • The US was also a top contender for India's largest trading partner in 2024-25 with $131.84 billion according to Indian government data reported by The Hindu on February 20, 2025, slightly exceeding the EU figure.
  • The claim uses 2024 data when 2025 data was available (Indian fiscal year 2024-25 data published February 2025 shows the US ahead).
  • The difference between EU and US trade volumes is very small (approximately €120 billion / ~$130 billion for EU vs $131.84 billion for US), making them effectively tied depending on exchange rates and exact time periods measured.
  • The European Commission source cited is from the EU itself, which has an institutional interest in emphasizing the EU-India relationship.
