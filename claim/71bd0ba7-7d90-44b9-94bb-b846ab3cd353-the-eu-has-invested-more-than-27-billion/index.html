The EU has invested more than 2.7 billion euros in cancer prevention, early diagnosis, detection and treatment over five years
92% confidence
HealthEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The specific figure of €2.7 billion refers to funds committed under Europe's Beating Cancer Plan as of October 2025, according to the European Court of Auditors. The Plan was initially launched with a €4 billion budget for 2021-2027 under the Multiannual Financial Framework.
  • The claim refers to the multi-annual Europe's Beating Cancer Plan launched in February 2021, which extends to 2027, not strictly a five-year period ending in 2026.
