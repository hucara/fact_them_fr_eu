Omissions
  • The claim uses slightly imprecise language ('affected') compared to the IMF's technical term ('exposed'). The IMF report notes that exposure does not necessarily mean job displacement—roughly half of exposed jobs in advanced economies may actually benefit from AI integration. The data is from January 2024 (IMF Staff Discussion Note SDN/EA/2024/001), not 2026, though this was the most recent IMF data available at the time of the January 2026 speech.
