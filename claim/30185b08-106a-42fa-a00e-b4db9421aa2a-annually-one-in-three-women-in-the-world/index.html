Annually, one in three women in the world experiences physical or sexual violence.
92% confidence
EqualityInternational
Omissions
  • The statistic refers to LIFETIME prevalence, not annual prevalence. The claim incorrectly states 'annually' when the data measures whether women have experienced violence at least once in their life.
  • The WHO 2023 report and UN Women data specify 'at least once in their life' or 'during their lifetime', which is substantially different from annual incidence.
  • The 840 million figure (approximately 27% of women aged 15-49) refers to intimate partner violence or non-partner sexual violence experienced over a woman's lifetime.
