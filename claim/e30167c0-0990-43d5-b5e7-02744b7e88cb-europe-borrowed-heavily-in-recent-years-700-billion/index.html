Omissions
  • The official authorized amount is €750 billion (2018 prices), not €700 billion
  • The borrowing is specifically for NextGenerationEU (NGEU), not general EU debt
  • The exact amount actually raised by March 2025 may be closer to €650-700 billion, with some funds still to be borrowed by end of 2026
  • This borrowing is exceptional and temporary, linked to post-pandemic recovery, not a permanent feature of EU finances
