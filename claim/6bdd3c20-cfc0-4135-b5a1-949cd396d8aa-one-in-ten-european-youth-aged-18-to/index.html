Overestimated
One in ten European youth aged 18 to 24 have abandoned education early.
92% confidence
EducationEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The claim does not specify the reference year; the most recent available data is from 2024, not 2026
  • The actual Eurostat figure for 2024 is 9.3-9.4%, which is lower than the claimed 10%
  • The claim omits the significant gender disparity: 10.9% of young men vs 7.7% of young women were early leavers in 2024
  • The EU has set a target to reduce early school leaving below 9% by 2030
