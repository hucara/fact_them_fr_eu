Omissions
  • The European Commission officially recognizes 9 candidate countries (not 10) as of early 2026
  • Türkiye's candidate status exists but negotiations have been frozen since 2018
  • Kosovo is considered a 'potential candidate' rather than an official candidate country
  • The EP Think Tank article from March 2026 confirms 9 candidates, though some sources include Türkiye to reach 10
