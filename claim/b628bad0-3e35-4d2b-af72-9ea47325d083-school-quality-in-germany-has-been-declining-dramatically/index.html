School quality in Germany has been declining dramatically since 2015.
85% confidence

Omissions
  • The decline was more pronounced between 2018-2022 than 2015-2018, with COVID-19 pandemic disruptions contributing significantly to the post-2018 drop
  • Germany's 2015 PISA results were historically strong, representing a high point after years of improvement following the 2001 'PISA shock'
  • The claim does not mention that Germany remains close to or at the OECD average in all three domains despite the decline
  • Source publication dates (2023-2025) are after the session date, though the data describes periods before 2026-02-11
