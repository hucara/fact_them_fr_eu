Omissions
  • The MEP understates the duration by about 10-12 years - Spain's Law 14/1994 on temporary employment agencies dates to June 1, 1994, which is over 30 years of existence by 2026.
  • The claim lacks specificity about which 'similar legislation' is being referenced - Spain's temporary employment agency framework has been in place since 1994, but has undergone several amendments.
  • The effectiveness claim is subjective and not quantified in the source material.
