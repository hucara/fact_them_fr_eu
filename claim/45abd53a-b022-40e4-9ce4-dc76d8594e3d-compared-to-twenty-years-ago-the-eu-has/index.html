Omissions
  • The comparison depends on whether nominal GDP (current prices) or purchasing power parity (PPP) is used; in PPP terms, the gap would be smaller.
  • Exchange rate fluctuations between the euro and US dollar significantly affect this comparison when using nominal GDP.
  • The claim could not be verified against primary sources due to search limitations; additional official sources would strengthen verification.
  • The EU expanded from 25 to 27 members during this period (Bulgaria and Romania joined in 2007), which affects historical comparability.
