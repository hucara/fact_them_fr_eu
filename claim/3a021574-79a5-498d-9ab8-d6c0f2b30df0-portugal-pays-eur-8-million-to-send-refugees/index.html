Portugal pays EUR 8 million to send refugees to other countries
75% confidence
MigrationPortugal
Omissions
  • The exact amount is approximately EUR 8.4 million (some sources cite EUR 8.8 million), not exactly 8 million
  • The mechanism concerns accepting asylum seekers from other EU countries (which Portugal is avoiding through payment), not sending Portuguese refugees to other countries
  • The payment is part of the EU Migration Pact's solidarity mechanism for 2026
  • Sources confirming the specific amount are journalistic reports; no official government or Commission source stating the exact figure of 8.4 million EUR was found
  • The payment corresponds to refusing approximately 420 asylum seekers under the solidarity pool arrangement
