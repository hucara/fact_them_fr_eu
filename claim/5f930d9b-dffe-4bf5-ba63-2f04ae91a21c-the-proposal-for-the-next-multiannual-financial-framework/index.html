The proposal for the next Multiannual Financial Framework includes a minimum 14% social investment commitment for all national plans.
92% confidence
EconomyEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The claim states the period as 2027-2033, but the actual Commission proposal covers 2028-2034, not 2027-2033.
  • The commitment applies specifically to the National and Regional Partnership Plans (NRPPs), which consolidate shared management funds.
  • The 14% allocation is for social objectives including skills enhancement, fighting poverty, and promoting social inclusion.
  • The proposal was presented by the Commission on 16 July 2025.
