Omissions
  • The 57% figure for adults combines both overweight (37%) and obese (approximately 20%) categories. The 8% figure for children under 5 specifically refers to overweight and obesity combined. The WHO European Regional Obesity Report 2022 was published in May 2022, covering data from 2017-2019 period for some indicators.
