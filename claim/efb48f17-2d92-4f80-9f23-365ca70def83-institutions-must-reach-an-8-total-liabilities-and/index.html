Omissions
  • The 8% threshold refers specifically to subordinated MREL resources as a percentage of Total Liabilities and Own Funds (TLOF), not the total MREL requirement itself
  • The SRF can only be accessed AFTER an 8% bail-in of total liabilities and own funds has been applied, not as a threshold for general access
  • The MEP omits that this is a bail-in precondition for using SRF funds in resolution, rather than a capital requirement to 'access' the fund in the traditional sense
  • The claim was made on 2026-03-25; the SRB MREL Policy 2024 is the relevant primary source
