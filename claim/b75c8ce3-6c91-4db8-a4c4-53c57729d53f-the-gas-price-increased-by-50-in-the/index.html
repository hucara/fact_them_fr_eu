Omissions
  • The 50% figure appears to refer to the Dutch TTF benchmark price (European reference) rather than specifically the Italian PSV price, though both markets are highly correlated.
  • The surge occurred during the first week of March 2026 (2-6 March), not exactly 'the last ten days' but rather a sudden spike in early March.
  • The price increase was driven by Qatar stopping LNG production due to Middle East conflict escalation, critical context for understanding the price movement.
  • Euronews reported a jump of up to 45%, while Ember reported 'nearly 50%' - the exact figure varies slightly by source and measurement period.
