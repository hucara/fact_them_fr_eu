Omissions
  • The statistic typically cited refers to 'meurtres à caractère idéologique' (ideological murders), not 'meurtres politiques' (political murders). These are different categories under French law and criminology.
  • The claim conflates two distinct categories: political assassinations (targeting political figures) vs. ideologically motivated violence (including racist, xenophobic attacks).
  • No official government or judicial source was found in the search results - sources found are journalistic or from advocacy platforms.
  • The time period and methodology for calculating this percentage are not clearly specified in available sources.
