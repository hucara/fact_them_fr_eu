Trade between the EU and Canada increased by 65% in less than ten years
85% confidence
EconomyInternational
Omissions
  • The 63-65% figure refers specifically to trade in goods, not total trade (goods + services). Total bilateral trade including services grew by over 70% according to some industry sources.
  • The specific period is 2017-2024 (7 years since CETA provisional application), not a full ten-year period.
  • The Consilium source was published in 2025, after the session date, though it contains data from 2017-2024 which the MEP could have known from earlier publications.
  • The figure of 63% is from an official EU source (Consilium), while the 65% claimed may represent rounding or a different methodological calculation.
