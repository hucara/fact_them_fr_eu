Serious restrictions on the use of nitrous oxide have already been introduced in the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, and Sweden.
92% confidence
HealthUnited Kingdom
Omissions
  • The UK ban on possession came into force on 8 November 2023 under the Misuse of Drugs Act 1971.
  • The Netherlands implemented comprehensive restrictions (production, sale, purchase and possession) following a legislative amendment announced on 9 December 2019.
  • Sweden's law limiting access to nitrous oxide entered into force on 1 July 2025, prohibiting sale when there is reason to believe it will be used for intoxication.
