Omissions
  • The claim rounds up the combined population figure. The actual combined population is approximately 1.91 billion (450 million EU + 1.46 billion India), not 2 billion.
  • The term 'consumers' is used loosely to refer to total population rather than strictly consumer-age or consumer-active population.
  • Population figures are based on 2025 estimates and may vary slightly depending on the source.
