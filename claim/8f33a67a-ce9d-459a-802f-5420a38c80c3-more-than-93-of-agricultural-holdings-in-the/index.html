Omissions
  • The official Eurostat figure states 'an estimated 93%' rather than 'more than 93%'. The data refers to the year 2020 from the Farm Structure Survey, which is the most recent comprehensive data available. The term 'family farm' follows specific Eurostat definitional criteria.
Sources
