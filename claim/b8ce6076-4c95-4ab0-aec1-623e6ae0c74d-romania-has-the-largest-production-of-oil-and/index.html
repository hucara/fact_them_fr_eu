Romania has the largest production of oil and gas in the European Union
75% confidence
Industry & EmploymentRomania
Omissions
  • The claim conflates gas and oil production. While Romania is the largest gas producer in the EU, it ranks third in oil production behind Italy and Denmark.
  • The claim does not specify whether it refers to combined hydrocarbon production or gas alone.
  • Data is from different years: gas production figures from 2025, oil production from 2022.
  • The MEP could not have cited the 2025 gas production data (published in early 2026) at the March 2026 session, though the data describes a period prior to the speech.
  • The claim omits that the Netherlands and Denmark remain significant producers, and that EU domestic production represents a small fraction of total EU consumption.
