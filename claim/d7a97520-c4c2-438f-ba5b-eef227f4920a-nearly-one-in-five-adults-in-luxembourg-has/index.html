Omissions
  • The most recent official data available from Eurostat/statbase.org is from 2022 (18.43%), not 2026
  • The OECD's 2025 Health at a Glance report indicates a trend toward approximately 19% obesity prevalence in Luxembourg, but exact 2026 figures were not yet available at the time of the claim
  • The figure represents BMI ≥30 kg/m², which is the WHO/Eurostat standard definition of obesity
  • Data published after March 12, 2026 session could not have been known by the MEP
