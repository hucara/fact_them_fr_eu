Omissions
  • The €726,000 figure represents total compensation including allowances (residence, entertainment, etc.), not base salary alone
  • The ECB's official Annual Accounts disclose a lower base salary figure (€466,000 for 2024, €492,000 for 2025)
  • The 56% higher figure comes from journalistic analysis adding various allowances, not from ECB's primary salary disclosure
  • Sources are from 2025 but reference 2024 data
