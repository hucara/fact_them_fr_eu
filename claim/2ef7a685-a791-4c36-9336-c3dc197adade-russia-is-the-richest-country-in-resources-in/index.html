Omissions
  • Russia is often ranked as #1 in the world for total natural resource wealth, not merely in the top three. Some rankings place it ahead of the United States (~$45 trillion) and Saudi Arabia (~$34.4 trillion). The claim may actually understate Russia's position.
  • The valuation of natural resources can vary depending on methodology (proved reserves vs. total estimated resources, market prices at time of valuation, types of resources included).
