Each year, EUR 300 billion of European savings are invested outside the European Union, particularly in the United States.
75% confidence
EconomyEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The figure originates from the Letta Report (April 2024), but investigative journalism has questioned the transparency of the underlying data source for this estimate.
  • The figure refers to 'diversion' of savings rather than direct measurement of outflows, and the exact calculation method is not publicly detailed in the report.
  • The Letta Report was published in April 2024, and the MEP spoke in February 2026; more recent data may be available but was not cited.
