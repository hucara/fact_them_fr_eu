Three million young people have already abandoned school and training without a diploma or higher qualification.
85% confidence
EducationEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The claim does not specify the age range (18-24) or the reference year (2024) for the statistic
  • The precise figure based on 9.4% of the EU 18-24 population (approximately 30-31 million) would be roughly 2.8-2.9 million, making 'three million' a rounded estimate
  • The data refers specifically to 'early leavers from education and training' as defined by Eurostat, which requires both low educational attainment AND not being in further education/training
  • The rate has declined significantly from 16.9% in 2002 to 9.4% in 2024, showing substantial progress on this indicator
Sources
