The cost of wind energy has fallen by 70% in a single decade
90% confidence
EnvironmentEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The 70% figure refers primarily to onshore wind; offshore wind has different cost reduction trends
  • IRENA data is global, not specifically EU - European trends may vary slightly
  • Cost reductions are measured using LCOE (Levelized Cost of Energy), a specific methodology that accounts for total lifecycle costs
  • The IRENA source used was published after the claim date (2024), though it contains historical data from periods prior to 2026-03-10 that would have been available to the MEP
