Omissions
  • The figure of 201 comes from a union survey (GSEE/ELINYAE), not from official ELSTAT statistics. Official ELSTAT data recorded 51 workplace fatalities in 2023, suggesting the survey uses broader definitions or different methodology.
  • The sources were published on January 9 and 14, 2026, shortly before the MEP's speech on January 22, 2026.
  • The figure represents 'at least 201' fatalities, indicating this may be a preliminary or minimum count.
