Omissions
  • The MEP did not specify the source of this comparison or define what constitutes a 'piracy case'
  • The 140 figure for Paris 2024 refers to cyberattacks reported by French authorities, which may use different measurement criteria than the 75 cases reported for Tokyo 2020
  • Other sources cite different figures for Tokyo 2020, including 450 million attempted cyber attacks reported by Fujitsu, suggesting different methodologies are used to count cyber incidents
  • The Tokyo 2020 Olympics were held in 2021 due to COVID-19 postponement
