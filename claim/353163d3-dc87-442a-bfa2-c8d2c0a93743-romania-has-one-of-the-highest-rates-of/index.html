Omissions
  • The MEP stated 'one of the highest' but Romania actually had THE highest rate in the EU in 2024, not just 'one of' the highest.
  • The data refers to 2024, which is the most recent complete year available before the session date of 2026-02-11. The data period covers young people aged 18-24 who had completed at most a lower secondary education and were not in further education or training.
  • The EU average for early school leaving in 2024 was 9.4%, making Romania's rate (16.8%) nearly double the EU average.
