Error detected
The claim incorrectly states that the EU Blocking Statute was used in 2012 under Barack Obama. In 2012, the EU was imposing its own sanctions on Iran alongside the US, not using the Blocking Statute to counter US sanctions. The statute was actually reactivated/updated in 2018 under the Trump administration, not 2012.
Omissions
  • The claim fails to mention the 2018 reactivation/major update of the Blocking Statute, which was the significant event involving the statute during the Iran sanctions episode. The 2012 date may refer to US sanctions imposed on Iran in 2012, but this did not trigger EU Blocking Statute activation at that time.
