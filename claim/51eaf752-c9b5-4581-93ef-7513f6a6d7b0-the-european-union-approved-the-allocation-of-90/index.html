Omissions
  • The claim was made on 21 January 2026, after the European Council political agreement (18 December 2025) but before the formal legislative approval by the Council of the EU (4 February 2026) and European Parliament (6 February 2026). The €90 billion figure covers 2026-2027, not 2026-2028 as implied by 'next two years' from the claim date.
  • The support is structured as a loan based on EU borrowing on capital markets, not a direct allocation of existing EU funds.
  • The European Council agreement represented a political commitment by EU heads of state, but the full institutional approval process was not completed until after the claim date.
