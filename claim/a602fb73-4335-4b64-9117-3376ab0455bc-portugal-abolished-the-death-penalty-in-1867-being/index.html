Portugal abolished the death penalty in 1867, being one of the first countries in the world to do so
95% confidence

Omissions
  • Venezuela was the first country to abolish the death penalty in 1863
  • San Marino abolished the death penalty in 1865 (for ordinary crimes), preceding Portugal
  • Portugal was specifically the third country to abolish the death penalty, not among the very first two
