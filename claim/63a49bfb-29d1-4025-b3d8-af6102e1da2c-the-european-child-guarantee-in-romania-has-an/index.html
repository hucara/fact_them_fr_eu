The European Child Guarantee in Romania has an allocation of approximately EUR 7 billion
85% confidence
Social PolicyRomania
Omissions
  • The EUR 7.3 billion figure represents the TOTAL ESF+ allocation for Romania for 2021-2027, not specifically the Child Guarantee allocation
  • The specific allocation for social inclusion (which includes Child Guarantee measures) in Romania is approximately EUR 1.9 billion from ESF+ funds
  • The Child Guarantee requires Member States with child poverty above EU average to allocate at least 5% of ESF+ funds to Child Guarantee-related measures
  • Romania's National Action Plan for the Child Guarantee commits to investing more than EUR 19 billion through a mix of EU and national funds, not only ESF+
