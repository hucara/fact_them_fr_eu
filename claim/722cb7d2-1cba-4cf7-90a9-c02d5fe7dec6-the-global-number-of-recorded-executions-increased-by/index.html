Omissions
  • The claim omits the absolute numbers: 1,518 executions in 2024 compared to 1,153 in 2023. It also omits that this is the highest number of recorded executions since 2015, and that the increase was driven primarily by Iran and Saudi Arabia, which accounted for 91% of all recorded executions. The data excludes China, where figures are classified as a state secret but estimated to be in the thousands annually.
  • The source (Amnesty International) was not cited by the MEP, though the figure is accurate.
