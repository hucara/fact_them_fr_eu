Omissions
  • The Venice Commission is an advisory body of the Council of Europe that issues 'opinions' and 'recommendations', not 'decrees'
  • The December 2025 amendment appears to relate to provisions on 'incitement to war' and possibly 'abuse of law' based on available sources, not specifically questioning Venice Commission opinions
  • Sources indicate the amendment was adopted by Parliament on December 11, 2025, but the claim states it was enforced 'last month in December 2025' which is imprecise
