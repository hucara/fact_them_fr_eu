Germany still pays the highest contribution to the EU
95% confidence
EconomyGermany
Omissions
  • The claim does not distinguish between gross contributions (total amount paid) and net contributions (amount paid minus EU funds received). Germany is the largest in both categories, but the net contribution figure is more commonly cited in analyses of member state contributions.
  • Contributions are calculated based on GNI (Gross National Income), meaning larger economies naturally contribute more. Germany's high contribution reflects its position as the EU's largest economy.
  • Germany's net contribution decreased by approximately €4 billion in 2024 compared to previous years due to economic challenges.
