Omissions
  • The MEP did not specify the reference period. The 'almost 50%' figure for house prices aligns with the 2010-2023 data (48%), while the 'almost 20%' for rents slightly understates the 2010-2023 figure (22%).
  • The Housing in Europe 2025 edition showing 53% price increase and 25% rent increase (2010-2024) was published after the session date but contains data from a period before March 2026.
  • Source is journalistic for the Facebook reference citing decade data.
