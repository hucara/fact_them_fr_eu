Omissions
  • The protest took place on December 17, 2025, not 'a month ago' from January 21, 2026 (which would be December 21, 2025).
  • The protest was specifically against amendments to Lithuania's public broadcaster (LRT) law that protesters feared would threaten media freedom and free speech.
  • The figure of 10,000 comes from LRT (Lithuanian National Radio and Television) and Euronews reporting.
  • A smaller protest of similar nature also took place on December 9, 2025.
