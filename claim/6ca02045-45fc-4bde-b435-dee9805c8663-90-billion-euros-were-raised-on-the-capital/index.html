Omissions
  • The €90 billion is a loan package approved for 2026-2027, not necessarily already raised on capital markets at the time of the claim on 11 February 2026. The claim was made shortly after Parliament's approval (6 February 2026), so it reflects the approved amount to be raised rather than funds already disbursed.
  • The financing is backed by the EU budget 'headroom' (margin between budget commitments and own resources ceiling) as guarantee.
  • The funding covers a two-year period (2026 and 2027) rather than a single year.
