Omissions
  • The MEP used outdated 2022 WHO data instead of the most recent 2025 data which shows a lower rate (25% vs 33%).
  • The 'nearly 1 in 3' (approx. 33%) claim refers to a 2022 WHO report, while the March 2026 claim should have cited the November 2025 WHO data showing 25%.
  • The data refers specifically to children aged 7-9 years in participating countries, not all children in the EU.
  • The WHO European Childhood Obesity Surveillance Initiative (COSI) does not cover all EU Member States.
