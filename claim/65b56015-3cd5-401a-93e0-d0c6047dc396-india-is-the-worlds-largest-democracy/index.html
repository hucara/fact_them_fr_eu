Omissions
  • While India is the largest democracy by population, some democracy indices (Freedom House, V-Dem) have noted concerning trends regarding democratic backsliding, press freedom restrictions, and minority rights in recent years. The classification of 'democracy' depends on the definition used—India ranks as a 'flawed democracy' in the EIU Democracy Index and 'partly free' in Freedom House's 2025 assessment, though still democratic in nature.
