Omissions
  • The European Media Freedom Act was published in the Official Journal and legally entered into force on 7 May 2024, but its core provisions only became applicable on 8 August 2025.
  • The distinction between legal entry into force (May 2024) and applicability of obligations (August 2025) is not mentioned.
  • The Regulation number is (EU) 2024/1083, adopted on 11 April 2024.
