Omissions
  • The claim does not specify the time period. The Mediterranean has been consistently the deadliest route in recent years (2024-2025), but this status should be contextualized with specific data.
  • The Central Mediterranean Route specifically accounts for the majority of Mediterranean deaths, not the entire Mediterranean equally.
  • While the Mediterranean is the deadliest single route in absolute numbers, the death rate per crossing attempt would provide additional context on relative danger.
