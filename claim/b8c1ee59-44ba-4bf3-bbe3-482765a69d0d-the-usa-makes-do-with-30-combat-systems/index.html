The USA makes do with 30 combat systems where Europe needs 178.
85% confidence
DefenceInternational
Omissions
  • The figures cited (30 vs 178) originate from European Commission data circa 2017, not current 2026 data
  • The 2021 European Parliament study cites the same figures, suggesting limited progress in standardization during this period
  • The comparison refers to 'major weapon systems' or 'weapons systems categories' selected by the Commission, not all combat systems
  • The source is a European Commission calculation based on selected weapons categories, not a comprehensive count of all military equipment
