Internal barriers are equivalent to tariffs of up to 40%
75% confidence
EconomyEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The most widely cited figure from the IMF is 44-45% for goods, slightly higher than the 40% claimed. The claim does not specify whether this refers to goods, services, or both. The IMF estimate of 44% represents an average, not a maximum ('up to'). The 40% figure may understate the average barrier level according to the primary IMF source. These estimates are contested by some economists who question the methodology used to calculate tariff equivalents.
