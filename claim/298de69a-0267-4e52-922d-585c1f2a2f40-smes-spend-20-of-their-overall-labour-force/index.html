78% confidence
Industry & EmploymentEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The figure of 20% is not supported by the available data from official or reputable sources; actual figures are substantially lower based on the most recent research available.
  • The KfW study cited is specific to Germany and may not represent the EU average, though Germany is often used as a benchmark for EU SME studies.
  • The claim blurs the distinction between administrative tasks broadly (7% of working time) and legal/regulatory compliance costs specifically (3.9% of personnel costs).
  • The session date is 2026-02-11; the most recent data available is from April 2025.
