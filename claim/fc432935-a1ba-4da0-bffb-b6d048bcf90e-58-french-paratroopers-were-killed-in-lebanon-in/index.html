58 French paratroopers were killed in Lebanon in 1983 in the Drakkar attack.
98% confidence
Foreign AffairsMiddle East
Omissions
  • The attack occurred on October 23, 1983, the same day as a similar attack on the U.S. Marine barracks in Beirut that killed 241 American servicemen.
  • The Drakkar building housed the 1st and 9th Parachute Regiments (1er and 9e Régiments de Chasseurs Parachutistes).
  • The death toll sometimes cited as 58 French soldiers excludes additional casualties (civilian casualties and injuries).
