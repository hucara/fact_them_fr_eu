The claim that U.S. industrial goods enter the EU 'duty-free' (0% tariffs) is factually incorrect - only approximately 67% of U.S. industrial goods face zero or very low tariffs
Omissions
  • The politician omitted that the EU applies Most Favoured Nation (MFN) tariffs on certain U.S. industrial products, not a blanket duty-free treatment
  • The 67% figure refers to 'zero or very low tariffs' - meaning some of these may have tariffs greater than 0% but below a low threshold
  • Context about the August 2025 EU-US trade framework was omitted, which established reciprocal tariff arrangements
