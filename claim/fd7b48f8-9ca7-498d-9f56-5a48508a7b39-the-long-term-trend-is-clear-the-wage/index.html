Omissions
  • The wage share shows cyclical patterns - it typically falls during economic expansions and rises during recessions (as seen in 2008-2009)
  • The decline has not been continuous; there have been periods of increase, particularly during economic downturns
  • The data available covers up to 2022-2024 period, before the claim date of February 2026
  • The trend varies significantly across EU member states
