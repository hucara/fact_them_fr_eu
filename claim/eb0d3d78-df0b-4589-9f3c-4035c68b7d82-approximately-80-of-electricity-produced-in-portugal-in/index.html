Overestimated
Approximately 80% of electricity produced in Portugal in 2025 came from renewable sources.
85% confidence
EnvironmentInternational
Omissions
  • The MEP conflated data from specific months (particularly January 2026, where renewables exceeded 80%) with annual 2025 data
  • The official 2025 annual figure from REN shows renewables accounted for approximately 68% of electricity consumption (27% hydro, 25% wind, 11% solar, 5% biomass)
  • The 80% figure reported by Euronews refers to January 2026, which occurred after the claimed period (2025)
  • Source REN article was published after the fecha_sesion (2026-03-25), but contains 2025 data the MEP could have referenced
