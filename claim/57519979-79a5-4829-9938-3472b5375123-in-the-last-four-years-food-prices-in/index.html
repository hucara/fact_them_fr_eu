Omissions
  • The data refers specifically to the period October 2021 to October 2025, which is a four-year span ending approximately five months before the claim was made. The figure of 24.9% was published by ISTAT in October 2025.
  • The increase was significantly higher than overall consumer price inflation, which was about 8 percentage points lower during the same period.
  • Il Sole 24 Ore headline refers to 'five years' while the data covers a four-year period from October 2021 to October 2025.
