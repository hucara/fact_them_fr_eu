Omissions
  • The figure of 2.7 million refers specifically to the EU Member States (EU-27), not the whole of Europe. The geographic scope 'Europe' is broader and includes non-EU countries, which would increase the total number.
  • The 2.7 million figure represents data for 2024 according to JRC estimates.
  • The figure shows a decreasing trend (-1.7% compared with 2022), which provides important context about cancer incidence trajectory.
Sources
