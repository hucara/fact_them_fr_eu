Omissions
  • The claim does not specify whether it refers to the EU or Europe as a whole. France is the 3rd largest economy in Europe by nominal GDP (after Germany and the United Kingdom), but 2nd largest within the EU-27.
  • The UK economy has been larger than France's since approximately 2022-2023, with a GDP of approximately $5.2 trillion compared to France's $4.0 trillion (nominal GDP, 2024 data).
  • Sources are from Wikipedia (secondary source citing IMF data) as direct Eurostat comparisons including non-EU countries like the UK were not immediately available.
