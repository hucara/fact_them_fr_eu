Omissions
  • The claim omits that the ~120,000 job losses occurred over a 12-month period, averaging approximately 10,000 per month, not tens of thousands monthly.
  • The data refers to the manufacturing sector specifically, which may differ from broader 'industry' definitions.
  • The Arbeitsagentur source was published on 2025-04-29, which the MEP could have known by the session date of 2026-02-11.
Sources
