Travellers must be refunded within 6 months in case of trader insolvency under the revised Package Travel Directive.
92% confidence
Internal AffairsEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The period was extended from the original 3 months to 6 months in the revision.
  • The 6-month deadline applies to payments from insolvency guarantee funds.
  • The revision includes additional provisions such as refund rights for linked travel arrangements and clearer rules for online bookings.
