Omissions
  • The EUR 6.3 billion figure is in current prices; the amount in constant prices would be EUR 5.6 billion
  • The Unity Safety Net represents approximately EUR 900 million per year over the 7-year MFF period (2028-2034)
  • The safety net is specifically designed as an EU-level instrument to address disturbances in agricultural markets, part of the National Regional Partnership Plans (NRPPs)
