The claim falsely states that children can change their sex without informing parents before age 16

The claim incorrectly suggests that children aged 6-12 can legally change their gender under Spanish law

The claim omits that parental consent is required for 14-16 year olds and judicial authorization for 12-14 year olds
Omissions
  • The law distinguishes between three age groups with different requirements
  • Parental consent is required for ages 14-16
  • Judicial authorization (not just personal choice) is required for ages 12-14
  • The law does not appear to permit gender marker changes for children under 12 years of age
