Omissions
  • The figures 516 out of 578 specifically refer to program creation and production procurements at Lithuanian National Radio and Television (LRT), not all public procurements in Lithuania
  • The audit covered LRT's procurements for program production only, not all public procurement sectors
  • The MEP's statement omits that this data relates specifically to the public broadcaster LRT, which could lead to the impression that these figures apply to the entire public procurement system in Lithuania
