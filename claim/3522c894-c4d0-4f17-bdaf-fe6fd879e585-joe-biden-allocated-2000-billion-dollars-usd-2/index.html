Omissions
  • The $2 trillion figure refers to the initial American Jobs Plan proposal (March 2021), not the final enacted legislation
  • The Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act actually passed was $1.2 trillion total ($550 billion in new spending)
  • Total industrial strategy investments across all major Biden-era legislation (Infrastructure Act, CHIPS Act, and Inflation Reduction Act) amounted to approximately $1.6 trillion, not $2 trillion
  • The CHIPS and Science Act added approximately $53 billion for semiconductor manufacturing
  • The Inflation Reduction Act added approximately $369 billion for clean energy and climate investments
