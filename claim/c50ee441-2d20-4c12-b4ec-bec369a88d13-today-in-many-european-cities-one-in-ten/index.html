Today, in many European cities, one in ten households dedicates more than 40% of their income to paying for housing
85% confidence
HousingEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The specific '10%' data corresponds specifically to cities (urban areas), not to the entire European Union. For the EU as a whole, the figure was 8.2% in 2024.
  • The original source (Eurostat) uses the term 'casi 10%' (almost 10%) for cities, not exactly 'one out of ten'.
  • The reference period is 2024, not 2026, since 2026 data would not yet be available in March 2026.
  • The indicator measures the proportion of population, not of households directly, although both concepts are closely related.
