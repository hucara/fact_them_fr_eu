The wine sector contributes EUR 130 billion to the European economy.
88% confidence
AgricultureEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The figure of EUR 130 billion refers to 2022 data, which was approximately 3-4 years old at the time of the claim in February 2026. The MEP did not specify this reference period.
  • The figure represents contribution to GDP specifically, not general economic contribution.
  • The data comes from a PwC study commissioned by CEEV (European Committee of Wine Companies), an industry association representing wine producers' interests.
