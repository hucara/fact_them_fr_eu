Omissions
  • The plant owners (Iberdrola and partners) have formally requested an extension to postpone the closure beyond 2027
  • Almaraz II is scheduled to close in 2028
  • The 2019 nuclear phase-out policy sets the closure of all Spanish nuclear plants between 2027-2035
  • There is ongoing debate and uncertainty about whether the closure will proceed as scheduled given energy security concerns and extension requests
