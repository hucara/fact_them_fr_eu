Trade has risen by more than 33%
92% confidence
EconomyEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The 33% figure mentioned by the speaker is conservative; more recent data shows significantly higher growth (63-71%).
  • The source that most closely approximates the 33% is the 2021 data showing a 33.7% growth compared to 2016, so the statement might be based on data from several years ago.
  • CETA was provisionally applied from September 2017, so the reference period varies according to sources (some use 2016, others 2017 as the base year).
