Omissions
  • The exact start date of the process is not precisely defined - the claim refers to 'turning words into actions' but doesn't specify when the 'words' began; European Parliament resolutions calling for IRGC designation were made repeatedly from 2023 onwards
  • The formal designation was made by the Council of the EU, not the European Parliament, following the EU's legal procedures for terrorist listings
  • The Guardian reported the designation on 29 January 2026, while the EU Council's official press release was dated 19 February 2026
