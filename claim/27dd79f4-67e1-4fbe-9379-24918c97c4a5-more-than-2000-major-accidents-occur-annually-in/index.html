Omissions
  • The MEP did not specify the reference year for the statistic
  • The most recent available data (2024) shows 1,507 accidents, significantly below the claimed 2,000
  • Historical data shows the figure above 2,000 was accurate for years like 2011 and 2014, but not for recent years
  • The figure of 1,507 represents 'significant railway accidents' as defined by ERA/Eurostat criteria
Sources
