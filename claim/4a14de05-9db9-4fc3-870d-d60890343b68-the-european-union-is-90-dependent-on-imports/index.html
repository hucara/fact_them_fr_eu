The European Union is 90% dependent on imports of rare earth metals.
40% confidence
Industry & EmploymentEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The consulted sources show that 95% of the EU's rare earth imports come from China, Malaysia, and Russia, but this does not equate to the percentage of total import dependence
  • No official figure has been found for the exact percentage of self-sufficiency or import dependence on rare earths in the EU
