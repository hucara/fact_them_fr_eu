Over 800 farmers close their businesses every day in the European Union

Omissions
  • The 'over 800 per day' figure is derived from a decadal average (3 million farms over 10 years), not current daily statistics
  • The data reflects net decline in farm numbers (including structural changes, consolidation, and retirement), not necessarily farmers 'closing their businesses' daily
  • The specific time period of the '3 million farms in a decade' statistic is not precisely specified in available sources
  • Recent data (2023-2025) on actual daily closure rates is not available in the sources found
