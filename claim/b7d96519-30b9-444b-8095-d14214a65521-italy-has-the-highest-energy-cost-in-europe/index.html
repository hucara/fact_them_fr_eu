The claim that Italy has THE highest energy cost in Europe is contradicted by official Eurostat data showing Germany and Ireland with higher household electricity prices in 2025

The absolute formulation ('the highest') is not supported by the data, which shows Italy is among the most expensive but not consistently ranked first
Omissions
  • The claim does not specify whether it refers to household or industrial electricity prices, or natural gas prices—each category has different rankings
  • The most recent Eurostat data available (first half of 2025) shows Germany, not Italy, had the highest household electricity prices
  • The claim lacks temporal specificity—energy prices fluctuate significantly by quarter and year
  • Data from Q4 2025 shows Ireland having higher household electricity prices than Italy
  • Italy's industrial energy costs are significantly above EU average (+30%) but not definitively the highest in Europe
