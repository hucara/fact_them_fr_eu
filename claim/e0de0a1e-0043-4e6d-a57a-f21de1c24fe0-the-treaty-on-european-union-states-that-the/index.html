Confirmed
The Treaty on European Union states that the European Council shall provide the Union with the necessary impetus for its development
99% confidence
Internal AffairsEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The MEP correctly quotes Article 15(1) of the TEU but omits the second part of the provision which states that the European Council 'shall define the general political directions and priorities thereof'. The complete sentence establishes both the impetus and the direction-setting roles of the European Council.
