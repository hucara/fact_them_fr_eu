Omissions
  • The EUR 500 billion figure refers to potential gains from closing the productivity gap with the US by one-quarter, not specifically from removing internal barriers
  • The figure represents potential economic gains rather than current losses
  • The ECB speech does not attribute this amount solely to 'internal barriers' but rather to broader structural reforms to boost productivity
  • The source is an ECB speech from February 2026, which was available before the March 11, 2026 parliamentary session
