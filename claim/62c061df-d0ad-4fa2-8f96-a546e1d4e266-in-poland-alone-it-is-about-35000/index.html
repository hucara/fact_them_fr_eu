Omissions
  • The MEP did not specify that the 35,000 figure is an estimate rather than official data
  • The most recent official count (February 2024) found 31,042 homeless people, which is about 11% lower than the claimed 35,000
  • The EAPN Poverty Watch 2025 report notes that official counts are underestimated and suggests a corrected total of approximately 53,400 people when accounting for undercounting
  • The figure of 35,000 appears to be closer to estimates cited in media reports (TVP World cited 'roughly 33,000') rather than official statistics
