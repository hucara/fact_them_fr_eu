Omissions
  • The claim does not specify which EU member states are being compared. While ICE's budget exceeds many smaller EU countries, it is significantly smaller than the defense budgets of major EU military powers like Germany (€95 billion), France (~€50 billion), Italy (~€30 billion), and Spain (~€15 billion). The comparison is technically accurate but selective.
  • The $12 billion ICE figure represents the base budget; additional congressional appropriations in 2025 may have increased this to approximately $29.9 billion for enforcement operations, which would exceed even more EU defense budgets.
  • Currency conversion rates fluctuate; the euro-dollar exchange rate affects precise comparisons.
