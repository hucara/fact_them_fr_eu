Omissions
  • Most sources citing the EUR 3.5 billion figure were published on or after February 10, 2026 (one day after the session date), though they reference estimates prepared by agricultural associations
  • The EUR 3.5 billion figure represents an upper estimate; some agricultural associations cited EUR 3 billion
  • The 75% and 80% figures appear to refer to specific crop losses in certain areas rather than overall regional losses, but the claim does not specify this context
  • The losses are attributed to storm systems ('borrascas') affecting Andalusia in early 2026, not just floods specifically
