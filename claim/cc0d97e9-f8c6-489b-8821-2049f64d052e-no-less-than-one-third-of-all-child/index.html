Omissions
  • The figure varies by year and source - some reports cite 30%, others 32%, and some up to 50% depending on the specific reporting period and methodology used by the IWF
  • The statistic refers specifically to CSAM reported to and processed by the Internet Watch Foundation, which may not capture all CSAM globally
  • Sources are from 2023-2025, but as of March 2026, this figure may have changed
  • Dutch news sources attribute this concentration to the Netherlands' extensive digital infrastructure, favorable hosting conditions, and privacy-focused regulations
