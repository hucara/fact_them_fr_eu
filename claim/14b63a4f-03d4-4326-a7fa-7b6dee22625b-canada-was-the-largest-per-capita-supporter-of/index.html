Omissions
  • The claim refers to 'end of 2025' but available sources only confirm this status as of August 24, 2025, not specifically at year-end
  • The data refers specifically to direct financial support, not total aid including military assistance
  • The source is a government press release (primary for Canada's position but potentially biased)
  • Other G7 countries may have increased contributions in the latter part of 2025 that are not captured in available sources
