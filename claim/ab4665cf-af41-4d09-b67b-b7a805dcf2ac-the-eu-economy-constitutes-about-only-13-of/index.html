The EU economy constitutes about only 13% of the global economy.
85% confidence
EconomyEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The MEP did not specify whether the figure refers to nominal GDP or PPP (Purchasing Power Parity), which produce different results (13.8% for PPP per IMF, nominal GDP share is slightly different)
  • The 13% figure appears to be rounded down from approximately 13.8% (IMF October 2025 data)
  • The data reflects a long-term declining trend that the MEP could have contextualized (EU share fell from ~27.5% in 1980 to ~14% in 2025)
  • Statista estimates approximately 14% for 2025, slightly higher than the 13% claimed
