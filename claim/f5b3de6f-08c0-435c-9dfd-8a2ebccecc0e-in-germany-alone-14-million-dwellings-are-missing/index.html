Omissions
  • The figure of 1.4 million specifically refers to affordable and social housing units (bezahlbare Wohnungen), not necessarily the total housing shortage across all market segments
  • The data comes from the Pestel-Institut research institute rather than official government statistics like Destatis
  • The claim was made on 2026-03-10; the data refers to end of 2024 estimates published in sources from early 2026, which would have been known to the MEP
  • Alternative estimates exist - DZ HYP (a mortgage bank) stated in October 2024 that 'There is no general housing shortage in Germany' with 43 million housing units available
