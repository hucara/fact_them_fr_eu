Women work two months more per year than men
75% confidence
EqualityEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The figure of 'two months' is an approximation that depends on how 'months' are calculated (working days vs calendar days, hours per day assumed).
  • Eurostat time use survey data from 2022 (most recent available) shows the gap varies significantly across EU member states.
  • The data refers to all unpaid work (domestic work, care for children and adults, volunteering), not only domestic housework.
  • Time use surveys have different reference years across EU countries (most recent data from 2022-2023).
  • The speaker did not specify the data source or reference year for the claim.
