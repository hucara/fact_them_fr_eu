Daily physical education is compulsory in schools, which contributes to the decrease in obesity and cardiovascular risks among young people

HealthHungary
Omissions
  • Hungary continues to face significant obesity challenges despite the daily PE mandate, with some sources indicating it has the highest obesity rate in Europe
  • The causal link between daily PE and decreased obesity is supported by fitness improvements but obesity prevalence data shows persistent challenges
  • The daily PE policy was implemented gradually from 2012 onwards, not immediately in all schools
  • Source publication dates (2024) are after the speech date (2026-03-12), but the data periods referenced (2012-2022) are within the valid timeframe
