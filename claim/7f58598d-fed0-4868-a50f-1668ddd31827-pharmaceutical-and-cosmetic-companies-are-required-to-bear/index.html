Pharmaceutical and cosmetic companies are required to bear 80% of the quaternary treatment and wastewater costs, potentially reaching 100%.
92% confidence
EnvironmentEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The requirement is part of the recast Urban Wastewater Treatment Directive (UWWTD), which was approved by the European Parliament in April 2024.
  • The 80% threshold applies specifically to the costs of removing micro-pollutants through quaternary treatment, not all wastewater treatment costs.
  • The implementation deadline for meeting this requirement is 31 December 2028.
