The European Union and Member States paid 88 billion euros in 2024 in development aid.
85% confidence
Foreign AffairsEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The most recent confirmed data available is for 2023, not 2024, as ODA statistics are typically published with a lag of several months; the figure of EUR 88 billion appears to understate actual EU collective ODA, which reached EUR 95.9 billion in 2023 according to the Council of the EU and EUR 96.5 billion according to the ONE Campaign.
  • The claim does not distinguish between ODA from EU Member States (EUR 82.45 billion in 2023 per CONCORD) and collective EU ODA including EU institutions (EUR 95.9 billion in 2023).
