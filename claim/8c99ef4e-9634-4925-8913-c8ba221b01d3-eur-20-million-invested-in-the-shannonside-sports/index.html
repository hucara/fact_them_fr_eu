Omissions
  • The claim conflates the total project cost (approximately EUR 20 million) with the specific PEACEPLUS funding amount (EUR 15.3 million). The PEACEPLUS contribution represents a significant portion but not the full amount.
  • The reference to 'Since 1995' appears to be an error. PEACEPLUS is a post-2020 program that succeeded the earlier PEACE and INTERREG programs. The EUR 15.3 million funding was approved recently (2024), not dating back to 1995.
  • The official project name is 'Shannonside Peace Arena' or 'Shannonside Recreational Campus', not simply 'Shannonside sports campus'.
  • The SEUPB (Special EU Programmes Body) is the managing authority for PEACEPLUS funding, not the European Parliament directly.
