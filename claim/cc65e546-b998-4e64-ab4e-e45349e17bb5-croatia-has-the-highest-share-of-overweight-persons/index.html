Croatia has the highest share of overweight persons in the European Union.
75% confidence
HealthCroatia
Omissions
  • The claim conflates 'overweight' (BMI 25-29.9) with 'overweight and obese' (BMI ≥25). Croatia's ranking varies depending on which metric is used.
  • According to the Eufic source, Romania, Poland, Croatia, and Slovakia share the highest prevalence, suggesting Croatia is among the highest but not uniquely the highest.
  • The data used is from 2019 or earlier (the Eurostat PDF appears to reference 2019 data), which was outdated by the 2026-03-12 claim date.
  • The MEP cited Eurostat but the specific claim about being 'the highest' appears to come from secondary journalistic sources rather than direct Eurostat publications.
