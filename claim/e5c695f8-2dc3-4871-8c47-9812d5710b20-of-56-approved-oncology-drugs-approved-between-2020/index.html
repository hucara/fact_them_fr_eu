Omissions
  • The specific figure of 56 oncology drugs could not be verified through available sources
  • The OECD report from September 2024 cites 46 oncology medicines, not 56, with 45 available in Germany and 1 in Malta
  • The EFPIA W.A.I.T. Indicator 2024 survey mentions 173 innovative medicines (all therapeutic areas) approved between 2020-2023, not specifically 56 oncology drugs
  • The MEP did not cite a source for these specific figures
  • Different methodologies for counting 'available' medicines (reimbursed vs. marketed vs. authorized) may explain the discrepancy
