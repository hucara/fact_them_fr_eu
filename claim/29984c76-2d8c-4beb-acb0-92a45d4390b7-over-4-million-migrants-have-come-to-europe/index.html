Over 4 million migrants have come to Europe illegally since 2015.
82% confidence
MigrationEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The figure refers to 'detections of irregular border crossings' not unique individuals - some migrants may be counted multiple times if they attempt crossing more than once
  • The data represents detections at EU external borders, not total migrants who successfully entered and remained in the EU illegally
  • The MEP used the term 'illegally' but Frontex officially uses 'irregular' to describe these crossings
  • Data for 2026 is partial as the session date is January 21, 2026
  • The figure includes multiple border crossing attempts by the same individuals, so the actual number of distinct persons is lower than the total number of detections
