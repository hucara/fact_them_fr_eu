There are EUR 10 trillion in savings idle in European bank accounts
95% confidence
EconomyEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The figure refers to household savings in bank deposits specifically, not all savings in European bank accounts (which could include corporate deposits)
  • The figure was first published by the European Commission in March 2025 as part of the Savings and Investments Union strategy announcement
  • The 10 trillion figure represents approximately 70% of total EU household savings, with the remainder held in other financial instruments
