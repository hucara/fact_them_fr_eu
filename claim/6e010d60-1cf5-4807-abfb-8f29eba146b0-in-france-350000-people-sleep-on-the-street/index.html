Social PolicyFrance
Omissions
  • The 350,000 figure for France includes not just people sleeping on the street but also those in temporary accommodation, squats, and other precarious housing situations (the 'sans-abri' and 'mal-logés' categories)
  • There is no harmonized EU-wide homelessness statistic that allows for a precise comparison of total homelessness across all EU member states using the same methodology
  • The Fondation Abbé Pierre report was published in October 2025, and the MEP could have cited this recent data
  • The 'four times more' claim appears to be an estimate without clear attribution to a specific source
