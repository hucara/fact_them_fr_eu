Omissions

  • The MEP did not specify whether the 63% referred to goods, services, or total trade
  • The source is journalistic; the primary source (European Commission) provides higher figures
  • The publication date of the European Commission source (March 5, 2026) is after the session date (March 10, 2026), but the MEP likely had access to earlier data showing similar trends
