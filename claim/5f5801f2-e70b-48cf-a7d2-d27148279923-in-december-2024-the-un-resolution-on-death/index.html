Omissions
  • The resolution was the 10th in a series of UN resolutions on death penalty moratorium, not the first resolution ever adopted on this topic. The claim appears to correctly identify this as the first time a two-thirds majority was achieved, but the phrasing 'the resolution was adopted for the first time' could be misleading without specifying 'with a two-thirds majority' as the novel aspect.
