Russia has deported and forcibly transferred 20,000 Ukrainian children.
85% confidence
Foreign AffairsEurope
Omissions
  • The figure of 20,000 comes from Ukrainian authorities and represents their estimate; precise independent verification of the exact number is difficult due to the ongoing conflict and lack of access to Russian-controlled territories.
  • The ICC arrest warrant from March 2023 specifically cited the war crime of illegal deportation of children but did not specify an exact number in the warrant itself.
  • Some sources indicate 'almost 20,000' or 'about 20,000' rather than an exact figure of 20,000.
  • The claim omits that the ICC has specifically classified these acts as war crimes, with broader investigations ongoing into potential crimes against humanity.
