Omissions
  • The 80% figure refers to Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) - raw materials used in drug manufacturing - not finished 'life-saving generic medicines'
  • The speaker conflates APIs with finished pharmaceutical products
  • The statistic covers all pharmaceutical production, not specifically 'life-saving' generics
  • The claim does not distinguish between generic and originator medicines in the API dependency figure
