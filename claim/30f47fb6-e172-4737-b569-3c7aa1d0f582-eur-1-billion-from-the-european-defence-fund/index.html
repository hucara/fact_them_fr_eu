EUR 1 billion from the European Defence Fund is being used for drone and counter-drone prototypes.
75% confidence
DefenceEuropean Union
Omissions
  • The EUR 1 billion figure refers to cumulative EDF funding for unmanned systems over multiple years (since 2021), not a single annual allocation specifically for drones.
  • The funding covers 71 projects including eight prototypes and twenty more planned, not solely counter-drone prototypes.
  • The European Commission announced over EUR 1 billion for the 2025 EDF Work Programme for general next-generation defence technologies, not exclusively for drones.
  • The European Defence Fund has a total budget of nearly EUR 7.3 billion for 2021-2027, with EUR 2.7 billion for research and EUR 5.3 billion for development and acquisition.
  • Source from euperspectives.eu was published on January 11, 2026 (after the session date of January 22, 2026 appears to be an error in the source - the article actually states January 2026 which would be before the session date).
  • The EUR 910 million figure for the 2024 EDF edition represents the annual call funding, separate from the cumulative drone-specific allocation.
